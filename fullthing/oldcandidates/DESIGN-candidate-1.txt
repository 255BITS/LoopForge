# DESIGN CANDIDATE 1: ContractLint
## AI-Powered Contract & Policy Validation SaaS

---

### Product Summary

A web service that allows freelancers, small agencies, and solopreneurs to upload contracts (NDAs, service agreements, terms of service) and receive instant validation against common legal pitfalls, inconsistencies, and red flags.

**Tagline:** "Lint your contracts before you sign."

### Risks & Mitigations

- **Legal liability:** Clear disclaimers - "not legal advice", educational tool only
- **Accuracy concerns:** Position as "first-pass filter", recommend lawyer for final review
- **Low conversion:** Aggressive free tier to build trust, case studies from beta users
